Services

Serving a wide veriaty of commercial customers

Coverage Area.

With a team of over 60 highly skilled technicians, we are equipped to provide comprehensive coverage across five states including Georgia, Alabama, Tennessee, Mississippi, and Florida. Our technicians are strategically positioned in hub locations in Mississippi, Georgia, Tennessee, and Florida, ensuring that we can respond quickly to any service requests and provide fast and efficient service to all our customers in the entire coverage area.

Commercial

Servicing a wide range of commercial properties, including big box retail stores, restaurants, and corporate facilities. Our goal is to provide our commercial clients with the peace of mind that comes with knowing that your facilities are in good hands, and that any issues will be resolved promptly and efficiently. With our capability to serve multiple states, you can simplify your plumbing needs by dealing with just one vendor instead of numerous ones.

Service Locations

We take pride in our extensive service capabilities! Having successfully serviced over 22,000 locations, we have proven our commitment to delivering top-notch service and expertise to a diverse range of clients.

Vendor Portal Compliance

At American Plumbing, we understand the crucial role technology plays in servicing commercial locations. Our commitment is to provide prompt updates and comply with all portal requirements, ensuring a consistent and seamless process for our clients. We offer a comprehensive solution, including multi-stage job updates, GPS tracking, portal notes, and check-in/check-out procedures, to meet your needs and provide peace of mind.
